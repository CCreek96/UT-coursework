About Us

Who We Are

Recipedia was started by two students at The University of Texas at Austin, Connor Creek and Katy McQuaid, in order to share delicious recipes in an easy and fun way.

How to Navigate Recipedia

To get around Recipedia, you can start by clicking on one of the topics on our home page, check out some of our most liked recipes by clicking on the Popular Recipes tab, or by using the search box located at the top of every page.

Questions, Comments, Concerns

We here at Recipedia value our users' feedback. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns in regards to our website please feel free to fill out the form located on the Feedback page or contact either Connor or Katy directly.
